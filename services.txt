Retrieve all todos for a user
GET/users/{user_name}/todos

Delete a todo of a user
DELETE/users/{username}/todos/{id}

Edit/Update a todo
PUT/users/{user_name}/todos/{todo_id}

Create a new todo
POST/users/{user_name}/todos/
